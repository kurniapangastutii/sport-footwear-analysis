# 💰 Sport Footwear Sales Performance Analysis

Overview

This project presents an interactive dashboard built using Power BI to analyze the sales performance of a multi-brand footwear company across different countries, sales channels, and discount strategies. 

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key business metrics such as total revenue, units sold, average price, and year-over-year (YoY) growth. It is designed to help stakeholders quickly understand performance trends, identify key revenue drivers, and support data-driven decision-making.

##🎯 Business Problems
The company is experiencing inconsistent sales performance over time, and it is unclear which factors are driving or limiting revenue growth. 

Key questions include:
What is causing fluctuations in revenue trends?
Which brands contribute the most to overall performance?
Does offering discounts effectively increase sales volume?
Which sales channels and markets should be prioritized?

Without clear insights, the company may struggle to optimize its strategy and achieve sustainable growth.

A line chart is used to visualize revenue trends over time. This helps identify fluctuations, growth patterns, and potential seasonality in sales performance.

Geographic Performance

##🔍 Key Insights

Fluctuating Revenue Trend

Revenue shows noticeable fluctuations over the years, including a significant drop followed by recovery. This indicates that the business lacks a consistent growth strategy and may be influenced by external factors or inconsistent campaigns.

Even Brand Contribution

All brands contribute relatively evenly to total revenue, with no single brand significantly outperforming others. This suggests weak brand differentiation and missed opportunities to leverage top-performing brands.

Limited Impact of Discounts

Increasing discount percentages does not lead to a significant rise in units sold. This indicates that higher discounts may not be an effective strategy and could reduce profitability without meaningful sales growth.

Balanced Sales Channels

Sales between online and retail channels are almost evenly distributed. While this shows diversification, it also suggests that the business has not fully optimized high-growth channels such as online platforms.

Global Market Presence

Sales are distributed across multiple countries, indicating a broad market reach. However, further analysis is needed to identify high-performing regions that can be scaled.

##🚀 Business Recommendations

Optimize Discount Strategy

Focus on identifying the most effective discount range instead of applying high discounts broadly. Targeted promotions can improve sales without sacrificing margins.

Strengthen Brand Positioning

Invest in marketing and promotion for top-performing brands to create stronger differentiation and drive higher revenue contribution.

Improve Sales Consistency

Develop a more structured sales strategy by leveraging historical trends, seasonality, and campaign planning to reduce revenue fluctuations.

Expand Online Channel Strategy

Enhance digital presence through marketing campaigns and improved user experience to increase online sales, which offers better scalability.

Focus on High-Potential Markets

Identify top-performing countries and allocate more resources to those regions to maximize growth opportunities.

##🧠Conclusion

This dashboard highlights that while the business maintains stable performance across multiple dimensions, it lacks strong growth drivers. By focusing on data-driven strategies in pricing, branding, and channel optimization, the company can achieve more consistent and scalable revenue growth.
